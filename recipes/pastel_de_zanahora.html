Receta de pastel de zanahoria

Description

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/2 cup raisins

Steps:

  1. Prepare the oven: preheat the oven to 180°C. Grease and flour a 23 cm diameter cake pan.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt.
  3. In another large bowl, mix the grated carrots, brown sugar, vegetable oil, and eggs. Beat until well combined.
  4. Add the flour mixture to the carrot mixture and mix well. Add the walnuts and raisins and mix again.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean.
  6. Remove from the oven and let cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then, unmold and let cool completely on a wire rack before serving.
